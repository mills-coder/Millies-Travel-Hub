Unit 1: Outcome 2

2.1

The use of varied IT tools aided me in completing my project. Most notably Visual Studio Code and Trello, these programs allowed me to both plan and complete my brief. The adaptability of Visual Studio Code to support a range of programs meant I could easily store my files in one location. Having one folder for this project that housed HTML and CSS, limited the back and forth and allowed me to keep on top of my tasks and identify if I had missed something. With the introduction of CSS to my project I changed how I approached HTML, inserting the relevant code to add the visual elements to my webpages. Furthermore, I am pleased with how Trello worked for this project. The labelling feature has allowed me to break up my cards into groups and easily identify which relates to which unit. As this project was independent, I changed my approach to what I had done previously with Trello whilst in a team. This meant controlling the board myself and making sure to update it when a task was being done/completed. I also did not need to mark out who had completed which task as I had completed it all myself.

2.3

When assessing the strengths and weaknesses of my project, I have looked at both my research and the final product. I am happy with the theme of my website, as multiple features evoke travel and holidays such as the emojis and colour scheme. This is what I intended with my website, by using blues and warm oranges I feel I have achieved those feelings. One of the main weaknesses is the lack of features. This is due to having limited time to complete the project, as well as limited training in HTML and CSS. If I had additional time to practice using these programs, I feel I would be able to further improve and include a range of new features. Moreover, with a short timeframe to finish this project I was limited to what I could include. If I had longer to complete it, I would have worked more on the layout of the pages, creating smaller boxes for each paragraph of text and shifting their positioning on the page. I would also have included buttons and further interactive features to make it more user friendly. I dislike the lack of images and would like to have added additional images that perhaps linked to other sites.
